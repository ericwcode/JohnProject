Ahlan Wa Sahlan! If you are here is because dance is a important part of your life. El Raqs Hayati was creatived to be a inspirational resource for dedicated dancers. The name of the festival means the dance is my life in Arabic. During these days you will learn and connect with dancers from around the world. Dance is a way to express our emotions through movements. Let's share our passion for dance together in New York City.

Munique Neith

Munique Neith is Brazilian from Arab descent and one of the most prestigious masters and belly dancers in the world, with years of International experience. Known by her didactical skills and her elegant dance, she travelled around the world teaching and performing belly dancing in international festivals in countries like Egypt, Brazil, Argentina, Chile, Mexico, Venezuela, Colombia, Costa Rica, Guatemala, Ecuador, Panama, United States, Mozambique, Morocco, Israel, Japan, China, Taiwan, Korea, Malaysia, Russia, Ukraine, Czech Republic, Poland, Serbia, Portugal, Switzerland, France, Germany, Italy and in the main cities in Spain. She is the director of Munique Neith’s Oriental Dance Academy, the biggest school in Europe exclusively for belly dancing, with more than 800 students. As a Master, she gives regular classes at her school, professional courses for teachers (in Barcelona, Portugal and Germany), a Master in Oriental Dance and she is responsible for the training of many actual recognized belly dancers in Spain and around the world. She is also the organizer of the prestigious Festival “Egipto en Barcelona” that takes place every year the first weekend in February.
